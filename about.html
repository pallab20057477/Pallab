About Rclms


RCLMS (Rabindranath College of Learning & Management Science) is the best Technical and Management college for the students to develop their skills after gaining an Undergraduate Degree in BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management) , BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) and BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) in Hospital Management. In the year 2000, the founder, “Mr. Pallab Das” had a deep vision to provide the best quality education to the students to produce result-oriented and skilled professionals to meet the ever growing demands of industries. Therefore, the college got the affiliation from "Vidya sagar university”, Govt. of West Bengal. This college has earned a huge recognition in the provision of the best quality education and the credit goes to the four pillers- Students, Parents, Teachers and Administration. The Management of the College has employed experienced Faculty members having maximum experience in their respective industries so that they can make the students eligible to take the challenges of the industry. As a result, these students have been placed globally in their desired industries respectively. The success of this college is an evident of the best quality education and a good placement record.
